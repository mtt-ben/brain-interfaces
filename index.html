01. Listen

Recording the brain's electrochemical symphony. Methods range from surface sensors to deep tissue probes.

  • EEG: Non-invasive surface electrical tracking.
  • fMRI: Mapping metabolic activity via blood flow.
  • Intracortical: Direct neuron recording via micro-arrays.

02. Talk

Sending signals back to modulate neural states or restore lost functions.

  • TMS: Magnetic pulses for non-invasive modulation.
  • DBS: Deep-seated electrodes for Parkinson's & Epilepsy.
  • Optogenetics: Light-based cellular control (Lab-only).

The ultimate frontier: Bidirectional systems that listen, analyze, and stimulate in real-time.
